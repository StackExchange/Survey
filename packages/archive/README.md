## Notes

- File names have been changed from their original public release.
- Years are labelled by the year results were released, not the year the survey was opened.
- Open/close dates for 2011–2013 reflect that the earliest surveys ran without a formal close.
- **2011 - 2015** didn't ship a `schema.csv`. **2016** was in the original year’s README and was extracted in April 2026 to a seperate file.
